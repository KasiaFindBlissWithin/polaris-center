Your Instructor

Dr. Sean Carey

Dr. Sean Carey knows that gap between the life you're living and the life you sense is possible — intimately. He spent years doing everything right on paper. A chiropractic practice that helped tens of thousands of people. Hundreds of personal development books — genuinely, hundreds. The seminars, the programs, the coaches, the deep and sincere commitment to becoming a better version of himself.

And still. The relationships never quite worked the way he hoped. The anxiety didn't leave. He found himself reaching for alcohol — not out of addiction, but escape. A way to quiet a restlessness inside him that nothing he'd tried could reach. From the outside his life looked like success. On the inside, something essential was blocked.

In 2015, in the middle of a spontaneous spiritual awakening he didn't know how to navigate, he encountered the Modern Mystery School — an ancient lineage of teachings passed down, teacher to student, without interruption, for over 3,000 years. He signed up skeptically, not knowing what to expect.

What followed changed the entire trajectory of his life. The restlessness he'd been medicating — gone. The anxiety — quiet, for the first time in his adult life. The relationship patterns that had repeated themselves no matter how much inner work he did — they began, finally, to break.

He hadn't found a better program. He had found something that worked at a completely different level. The soul.

Dr. Sean has spent every year since learning these tools more deeply, and passing them on to people who are ready. He brings 15 years of clinical discipline as a chiropractor to how he holds space — grounded, precise, present. But what matters most cannot be credentialed. He has lived by what he teaches and been transformed by these same tools. And he is coming to Austin to share that same gift with you.
