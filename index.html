Origins · Over 3,000 Years

The Temple of King Salomon

More than 3,000 years ago, King Salomon gathered together 40,000 healers, warriors, shamans, oracles, and medicine people from around the world. They worked for around 30 years in a sacred Temple space — searching for a universal system that activates our Divine Essence and works on all humans, regardless of where they come from on the planet.

King Salomon wanted to empower all humans — to move humanity from stagnation and suffering into progression, unity, and peace. To create Shamballah, or Peace on Earth. World Peace starts with each individual. As we create peace within, we can share it with those around us.

Our lineage is over 3,000 years old as far as records reveal, but reaches back over 8,000 years to the roots of Hermetic Wisdom — traceable to King David, then Enoch, then Abraham, and Adam.

Ancient Wisdom for Modern Life

What is a Mystery School?

A Mystery School can be likened to a tree with a strong trunk and several branches. Each has a foundation in its own lineage and different branches of study — Shamanism, Alchemy, Druidism, and more.

Mystery schools have historically kept their teachings secret for three key reasons: protection of sacred knowledge, ensuring the spiritual readiness of the student, and maintaining the purity of the ancient teachings.

The common misconception is that the mystery is hidden "out there." It is not. It is hidden within each of us. The teachings help us look within to discover hidden resources, strengths, gifts, and talents — to Know Thyself, and to fulfil our life purpose.

  • ✦The teachings contain NO religion and NO dogma
